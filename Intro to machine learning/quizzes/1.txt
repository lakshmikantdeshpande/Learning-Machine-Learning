Q. Which ones are the examples of supervised classification?

1. from an album of tagged photos, recognize someone in a picture
2. analyze bank data for weird looking transactions, and flag those for fraud
3. given someone's music choices, and a bunch of features of that music (tempo, genre), recommend a new song 
4. cluster udacity students into types based on learning styles.

A: 1, 3 are examples of supervised classification, whereas 2, 4 are examples of unsupervised classification
